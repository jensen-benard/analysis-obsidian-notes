## Statement

Let $A$, $B$, $C$ be sets.

If $A \subseteq B$ and $B \subseteq A$, then $A = B$.

## Proof

By [[Definition - subset]], 
$$x \in A \implies x \in B$$
for every object $x$.

Similarly,
$$x \in B \implies x \in A$$
Hence, 
$$x \in A \iff x \in B$$
By [[Definition - equality of sets]],
$$A = B$$
